The End Of Creator

~Book by SHEKHAR KHANDUJA



SUMMARY

Do you believe in the creator? Or alien invasions per say? Or do you believe in afterlife? Yes, no, maybe? Doesn’t matter if you are a fan of science fiction, psychology thrillers or even pulp fiction, mark your territory. This book ‘The End Of Creater’ has so much to offer in just 152 pages and the plot will blow you mind.The author of this book Mr. Shekhar Khanduja is an IITian who has designed this book in such a way that it will definitely take your breath away. It is a perfect mixture of thrill, fiction, science, psychology and terror. His expertise at delivering these emotions is indeed commendable.

The End of Creator starts off with a handsome guy, Tod who, with his beautiful girlfriend is going to a party in a fancy car . Both of them are very rich and have poshe backgrounds. However, while coming back they are faced with a terrible accident where the protagonist, Tod dies. The story just begins here as the truths and the mysteries of afterlife are unravelled. He sees himself standing in a tunnel which leads to a beautiful afterlife palace that coincidently is headed by his very own grandfather. There is a threat to humanity. No more humans will be produced. The outcomes will be devastating. Humanity is at its worst stage. There is no one who can save them from earth. It is only the crew of these dead men and women in whose hands now lies the big responsibility of saving the planet earth.

The author indeed has a very vivid and inexplicably insane imagination and he also has the power to make us believe in this vast world crested inside his head. The book is not only a visual treat but also a souvenir to the person who keeps the visual imagination intact till his death. It is the only book that can indeed make you go head over heels about after life and how cool it can be up there. The mystery is worth your read and one is bound to be amazed at what can a person’s imagination be. The suspense of the mystery is breath-taking and the thrill can I deed send chills down your spine. Have you thought about how your afterlife would be? For most of us, it would be driven by the religious and cultural factors associated with it but trust me this book has the power to alter your perspective of how your afterlife will be.

The excitement of this book not only lies in its content but also in the way it has been said. Little can one imagine that the concept of heaven and hell are so outdate now that god created a unanimous warrior retreat where all who can protect humanity reside and train. One can easily ponder upon the fact that what of all the people who die on earth are sent to another planet to protect our earth from the far residing aliens? And now imagine that a guy, Tod gets to be in charge of one such projects.

In the end, it is indeed worth giving a shot and the book will not disappoint you if you are a visual reader. You are bound to dream about space afterlife and aliens after finishing it and trust us our crew can indeed vouch on this fact. Are you ready to come with us on this cool journey to the after-life planet? If yes, we welcome you aboard.
